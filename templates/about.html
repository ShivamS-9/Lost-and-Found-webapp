About

Welcome to our online community for IIIT students! We understand that students have busy lives, and sometimes things get lost or misplaced. That's why we created this website to help students find their lost items, borrow or buy items they need, and sell items they no longer need.

Our community is made up of IIIT students, so you're bound to find something you need or want. Here are some of the features of our website

Lost and Found

If you've lost something on campus, don't worry! You can post a listing on our post page with a description of the item and imag if possible. Also if you came up with something around the campus that you think someone has lost, go ahead post it on the post page.

Borrow

If you need to borrow something for a short period of time, you can browse through our listings of items available for borrowing. Maybe you need a textbook for a class, or you need a bike to get around campus. Other students may have what you need and be willing to lend it to you.

Sell

If you have something you no longer need, you can post a listing on our website to sell it to other students. This is a great way to make some extra cash and help out your fellow students at the same time. You can list items such as textbooks, furniture, electronics, and more.


Our website is completely free to use, and we encourage you to join our community of college students. Whether you're looking for something specific, or you just want to browse through the listings, we're here to help. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.
